banana seasonal changes



Here are some insights into the seasonal changes and growth stages of banana:

1. spring planting changes

In spring, banana are planted in warm, sunny soil. Early growth focuses on root development and small shoots.

2. summer: flowering and fruit development


Summer is the peak growth phase for banana. Flowers bloom, and fruits start to develop and ripen with increased sunlight.

3. fall: harvesting and ripening


In fall, banana are harvested. The fruits reach their full ripeness, and the plant starts to slow down as the weather cools.

4. winter: dormancy or indoor growing


In colder climates, banana may go dormant during winter or be grown indoors in controlled environments.
